Promoting healthier living by gamifying getting up and out of your house

  1. Press PLAY to start a new game
  2. You will be given a list of items to find
  3. Use your phone camera to take pictures of items
  4. You will have 20 minutes to find as many items as you can
  5. Microsoft Computer Vision will be utilized to validate pictures match the given items
  6. Share your score with friends and family!
